how to be friend? how to befriend?

what may colloquially be called friendship, ultimately, is not one fixed thing and definitely not an easy one to comprehend. filled with sass, rivalry, violence, and tenderness, freund is an exploration of the bonds between two people.

the duet questions what makes or breaks a queer man. both performers draw upon their experiences of having been socialised as a male, or rather, into becoming a male. this process leaves unabandonable traces. however, there exists the possibility of reframing, reclaiming and writing one’s present not as a slave of one’s past.

„In our mini skirts, drinking champagne till it hurts, oh yeah

You know you always will be my girl

Tryna pull us into the real world

You know these lonely people hate this

The way we’re always hand in hand, side by side

G-g-g-got our matching Juicy lockets

Keep our celly in our pockets

You’re my sexy little BFF“

performed February 2026 at MUK.Theater, Vienna (AT) and June 2026 at SHARE.festival, Bratislava (SK)

concept and choreography: Jonah Martensen and Moritz Grossmann

music: BFF by Slayyyter (feat. Ayesha Erotica), the city is calm at this hour by miffle
