PROJECTS AGENDA ABOUT

freund is a study in image, duration, and the afterimage that lingers when a scene slips out of frame.

The work asks how a body can remain visible long after the camera has passed. Memory becomes a second structure, overlapping the first one like a soft echo.

Each gesture is held just long enough for its atmosphere to become legible, and each stillness carries the pressure of what might happen next.

small notes, quick fragments, and suspended details gathered during the process.

the frame breathes slowly, the light shifts, and the image accumulates its own private history.
