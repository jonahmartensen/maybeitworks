a performative parcours through bodily textures and gestures.

the rose in both its white and red form is a potent alchimist symbol. through its power we can transform our current way of being human. a curiosity for transcendental potential leads the performers on a parcours of play, scourge, siblinghood, and desire.

performed 29th June 2024 at fusion festival Laerz

special thanks to Emil Torp
